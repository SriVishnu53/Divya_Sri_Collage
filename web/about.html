About
Divya Sri College Of Arts & Science

Divya Sri College of Arts and Science is a leading educational institution that offers high-quality education to students across various disciplines. The college is committed to providing holistic education that nurtures intellectual, moral, and social growth.

With a rich legacy of academic excellence, the college emphasizes values of integrity, inclusivity, and community service, preparing students to make meaningful contributions to society.

History

Established in 2005, Divya Sri College aims to provide accessible education to all. Over the years, it has grown into a prestigious institution recognized for its academic rigor and student welfare initiatives.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver a holistic education that nurtures critical thinking, creativity, and leadership. We strive to prepare responsible citizens and future leaders.

Core Values

Ethical values, respect for diversity, and social responsibility are at the heart of our institution. We aim to instill these principles in every student.

Facilities

Modern classrooms, laboratories, a well-stocked library, and recreational spaces provide a conducive learning environment for our students.

Sports

Our college offers a wide range of sports activities, including basketball, football, cricket, and athletics. We focus on promoting physical fitness and teamwork among students.
