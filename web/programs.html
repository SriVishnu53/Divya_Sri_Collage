BA (Tamil)

The BA Tamil program offers a deep dive into Tamil literature, history, and culture. It provides a foundation for careers in teaching, research, and creative writing.

BA (English)

BA English focuses on enhancing linguistic proficiency and analytical skills through the study of English literature, language, and communication. Graduates excel in media, teaching, and public relations.

BA (Maths)

The BA Mathematics program combines theoretical concepts and practical applications, preparing students for careers in teaching, data analysis, and mathematical research.

B.Com

B.Com offers in-depth knowledge of finance, accounting, and business management. It paves the way for careers in banking, auditing, and entrepreneurship.

B.Com (CA)

B.Com (Computer Applications) integrates commerce with IT skills, enabling students to excel in areas like e-commerce, digital marketing, and software development.

BCA (Computer Application)

The BCA program equips students with essential knowledge in programming, database management, and software development, preparing them for careers in IT and software engineering.

B.Sc (Computer Science)

The B.Sc Computer Science program focuses on advanced computing, algorithms, and system design, opening opportunities in AI, machine learning, and software development.
